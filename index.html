About Brewdog

In April 2007, in a godforsaken industrial estate in north east Scotland, BrewDog came howling into the world. Boring flavourless lagers and stuffy ales made Brewdog decide to fix that sorry state of UK beer market.

Nevertheless their biggest mission was to make other people as passionate about craft beer as they were. And that is still Brewdog's biggest mission today.
